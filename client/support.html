Return Policy Overview

At ShopMate Algeria, customer satisfaction is our priority. We offer a comprehensive return policy to ensure you're completely happy with your purchase.

Return Eligibility

✅ Eligible for Return

  • Items in original condition
  • Original packaging included
  • All accessories and manuals
  • Within 7 days of delivery
  • Unused and undamaged items

❌ Not Eligible for Return

  • Used or damaged items
  • Items without original packaging
  • Personal care items
  • Custom or personalized products
  • Items beyond 7-day window

Return Process Details

Step 1: Contact Our Support Team

Email us at shopemate.dz@gmail.com or call (+213) 669 453 240 with your order number and reason for return.

Step 2: Return Authorization

We'll provide you with a Return Authorization (RA) number and return shipping instructions within 24 hours.

Step 3: Package Your Return

Pack the item securely in its original packaging, include all accessories, and attach the RA number to the package.

Step 4: Ship the Return

Send the package to our return center using the provided shipping label. Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility unless the item was defective.

Refund Information

Refund Timeline

  • Processing: 2-3 business days after we receive your return
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • Credit Card: 5-10 business days
  • Cash Refund: Available at our Setif office

Refund Methods

  • Original payment method (preferred)
  • Bank transfer to your account
  • Store credit for future purchases
  • Cash refund (in-person at our office)

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item:

  • Contact us immediately with photos of the damage
  • We'll arrange pickup at no cost to you
  • Replacement or full refund will be processed immediately
  • Express shipping for replacement items

Exchange Policy

We currently don't offer direct exchanges. To exchange an item:

  1. Return the original item following our return process
  2. Place a new order for the desired item
  3. We'll prioritize processing your new order once the return is received
